Who We Are

This is an online consultation platform that fastens the
diagnosis of STIs through predictive analysis of symptoms keyed in by the patient.

How We Operate

The patient keys in their symptoms and the system matches it to the most accurate
disease from the database. It then prescribes the relevant medication or refers you
to a doctor. Alternatively, a patient can directly consult a doctor online.
